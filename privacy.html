Drivaro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-10

Drivaro respects your privacy and processes as little personal data as possible. This policy explains what data may be processed and why.

1. Data We Process

  • Trip data (date, odometer values, locations, times, trip type, notes)
  • Vehicle profile data (such as license plate and vehicle description)
  • User settings (language, unit preferences)
  • Account data (email address) for authentication

2. Local Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device.

3. Cloud Synchronization

If you use cloud sync, your trip and related app data may be stored in our backend service (Supabase) to synchronize across devices.

4. Location Data

Drivaro only uses location when you explicitly tap location detection buttons in the app. Drivaro does not run continuous background location tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only use technical service providers required for app functionality.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained while you use the app, or until you delete it in-app or remove your account.

7. Your Rights

  • Delete trips in the app
  • Clear local app data
  • Delete your account (see Account Deletion page)

8. Contact

For privacy questions: huizelarsen@gmail.com

Account deletion: See Account Deletion.

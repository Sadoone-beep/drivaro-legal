Drivaro Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-03-10

If you created a Drivaro account, you can delete your account directly in the app.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Drivaro
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap Delete account
  4. Confirm deletion

What is deleted

  • Your authentication account
  • Your cloud-stored trip data linked to your account
  • Local app data on the device where deletion is performed

What may remain

  • Data exported by you manually (e.g., Excel files) outside the app
  • Temporary technical logs retained by infrastructure providers for security/operations (limited retention)
